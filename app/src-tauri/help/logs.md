# :icon[logs] Communication Logs :toolbar

Click on the Toolbar :icon[logs] button to view HTTP reads and writes.  This log is global for Apicize, it reflects communication for
all active Workspaces.

:image[logs.webp]

You can clear the logs by clicking on the :icon[clear] button.

## Follow (Tail)

The "Follow Log" checkbox at the top of the screen will automatically scroll to the most recent activity when checked.
